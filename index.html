About Me

Hi! I'm Avani Shitole, a passionate Product Manager at Airtel Digital. I earned my MBA from MICA (Class of 2021) and bring experience from dynamic startups like Foxy and established brands like Titan. I thrive on transforming user insights into compelling products, leading cross‑functional teams and crafting strategies that balance business goals with delightful user experiences. When I'm not iterating on product roadmaps, you might find me exploring natural language processing, sketching UI ideas or doting on my mischievous cat.

I speak multiple languages, love to travel and believe that empathy and curiosity are the cornerstones of effective product design. This portfolio showcases my journey and the projects I've led—thanks for stopping by!
